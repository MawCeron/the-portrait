## Key Plot Points

### Part One: The Commission (1503-1504)

1. Francesco's social humiliation at Palazzo Strozzi dinner (new addition)
2. Decision to commission portrait from Leonardo
3. Lisa's sittings - her experience of being studied
4. Leonardo's distractions and Francesco's growing fascination
5. Leonardo leaves Florence with unfinished portrait

### Part Two: The Chase Begins (1504-1510)

6. Florence: Francesco discovers Leonardo is more than a painter
7. Milan: First journey, the osteria conversation, returns empty-handed but glowing
8. Pattern establishes: Francesco's glow after visits, then gloom at home (needs expansion)
9. Lisa's reckoning: realizes it's about Leonardo, not the portrait
10. Lisa's erosion: psychological cost, marriage strain (needs expansion)

### Part Three: The Grand Tour (1513-1516)

11. Rome: The Pope Dodge - Leonardo walks him into papal chambers
12. The Conversation: Lisa tries to convince him to give up, Medici orders increase
13. The Rumors: Leonardo in France, Francesco quieter, something has changed
14. The Quiet Years: Francesco asks if he's too old to go to France
15. Camilla dies (1518) - not explicitly stated yet, but changes Francesco

### Part Four: The End of the Chase (1519)

16. The Final Journey: Francesco decides to go to France (driven by Camilla's death)
17. Too Late: Arrives May 2, 1519 - Leonardo died that morning
18. The Portrait At Last: Melzi shows him the painting, Francesco leaves it there
19. The Return: Francesco devastated, "I wasted my life"
20. **The Reveal:** "It was Camilla again" - full story of daughter's death, parallel griefs
21. The Letter Arrives: Francesco refuses to read it
22. The Reading: Lisa reads Leonardo's letter alone
23. The Revelation: Francesco reads letter, finds peace
24. Epilogue: Lisa reflects years later, portrait in France, true masterpiece was the friendship
