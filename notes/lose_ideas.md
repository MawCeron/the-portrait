## Symbols and Motifs

**The Portrait:**

- Object of pursuit that's really about connection
- Bridge between two men
- Lisa made immortal but as enigma, not as herself
- Unfinished like everything Leonardo touched
- More valuable uncaught than caught

**The Chase:**

- Francesco's identity and purpose
- What gives his life meaning beyond commerce
- The journey more valuable than destination
- Parallel to Leonardo's perfectionism
- Both men unable to let things be finished

**Distance and Arrival:**

- Always arriving too late
- Three hours too late (Camilla and Leonardo)
- "Someday" that never arrives
- Geography as metaphor for emotional distance

**Letters:**

- Leonardo's letter as final gift
- Written words outlasting spoken ones
- Francesco keeping it in his old ledger (joining two halves of his life)

**Hands:**

- Merchant's hands vs. artist's hands
- Lisa's folded hands in portrait
- Francesco's aging hands
- Leonardo's paralyzed hands in final years
