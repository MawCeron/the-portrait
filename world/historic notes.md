### Key Historical Facts:

- Marriage: Francesco and Lisa married around 1495 (she was 15)
- Children: Three living sons (Piero, Andrea, Giocondo), two daughters
- Camilla became Suor Beatrice, died 1518 at San Domenico di Cafaggio
- Marietta becomes a nun in 1521 (after the main narrative ends)
- Portrait commissioned 1503, never delivered
- Leonardo died May 2, 1519 at Château du Clos Lucé, France
