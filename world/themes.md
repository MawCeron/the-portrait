## Core Themes

1. **The Chase vs. The Catch:** What we pursue matters more than what we possess
2. **Obsession as Identity:** Francesco defines himself through the pursuit
3. **Being Seen:** Both men needed to feel valued by someone who understood them
4. **The Cost of Ambition:** What Francesco sacrificed (family, presence) for transcendence
5. **Class and Recognition:** Merchant trying to transcend his station through culture
6. **Completion vs. Perfection:** Both men struggle with finishing things
7. **Love and Understanding:** Lisa watching, knowing, loving Francesco through it all
